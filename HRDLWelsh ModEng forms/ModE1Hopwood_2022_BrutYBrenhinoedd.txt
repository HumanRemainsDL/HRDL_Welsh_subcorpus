Because their ancestors before then had wrongfully made the Britons taxable to them and the Britons refused this tax which was unduly being pursued by them. And when Arthur had obtained the victory he ordered the dividing of the corpses of his own men and those of his enemies and to treat them with royal ceremony and to take them to the monasteries established in their [own] countries and to bury them there with honour. And so Bedwyr's body was taken to his own city in Normandy.
